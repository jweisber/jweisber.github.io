Reducing PhD Programs in Philosophy
Jan 1, 0001

We think philosophy PhD programs should be reduced by about one half. Instead of producing around 3x as many PhDs as there are tenure-track jobs available in academic philosophy, we suggest 1.25x or 1.5x as a target. We don’t mean shuttering half the PhD programs out there, necessarily. Each program could just train fewer PhDs. MA admissions could be expanded accordingly, for programs that need the TA labour.
